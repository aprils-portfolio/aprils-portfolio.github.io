About

Me, the creator.

I'm April! I'm a freshman in high school. I don't own a camera (yet), so these were the best I could do on my phone.
I enjoy taking pictures for fun, just for when I travel or see a pretty landscape, etc. I don't plan on ever taking photography professional, but I do hope to get a camera soon!
I first started getting into photography when my friend and I joined a media club in school. We were provided any and all supplies necessary to take pictures. Having all this professional equipment at my reach, I truly began enjoying photography.
Even after the club ended, I found myself putting more effort into my photos.
This website was created using HTML and CSS code that I learned through summer camp. I am still finding new ways to develop and enhance it further.
